2C) The innovation,VIVE VR, is a game system that plays VR games on PC. VIVE VR is beneficial because it is safer than other VR systems. It is safer since the system has a Chaperone System(1), which tells you if you are about to bump into a wall and allows you to continue playing with the VIVE VR system, with the feeling of being safe. Also the front facing camera allows the user to look around the "real world" so the user can get something to eat or talk to their friends(1). One possible harmful effect is that the user can experience cyber-sickness(2). Cyber-sickness is a physical side-effect that causes nausea, headaches, eye strain and neck strain(2). One possible impact to society is pain-management.This impacts society because VR has a palliative effect which allows cancer patients to feel less pain and the reduction of pain can boost survival rates(2).
